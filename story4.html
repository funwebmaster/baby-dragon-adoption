First Flight

By: Tavern Tabletop - Alison Holloway

The baby dragon dreams of soaring through the skies like the elder dragons,
but every attempt at flying ends in an amusing tumble.
With the help of its human caretaker,
the dragon practices day and night.
When an unexpected storm forces the young dragon to take flight
to save a friend, it discovers that courage, not just wings,
is what makes a dragon truly fly.
