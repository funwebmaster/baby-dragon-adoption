Baby Dragon Care Instructions

Home Care Stories Games

Congratulations on adopting your very own baby dragon! These magical creatures are full of wonder, curiosity, and adventure. Here are some important guidelines to ensure your dragon is happy and well cared for:

Feeding

Baby dragons love imaginative meals! Create a diet of make-believe treats like fireberries, enchanted marshmallows, and golden apples.

Some dragons enjoy tiny, real snacks like fruit slices or crackers (with permission from a grown-up).

Playtime & Training

Dragons love to explore! Take your baby dragon on imaginary flights around your home or yard.

Teach it fun tricks like ‘flame breath’ (blowing warm air) or ‘wing flaps’ (gentle hand movements).

Read your dragon bedtime stories—they love fairy tales and legends!

Rest & Comfort

Create a cozy nest using soft pillows or blankets for your dragon to rest.

Sing lullabies or tell stories before bedtime to keep your dragon feeling safe and loved.

Bonding & Magic

Your baby dragon thrives on friendship! Talk to it, share secrets, and go on pretend quests together.

Draw pictures or write about your dragon’s latest adventures.

Keep a Dragon Journal to document all the magical moments you share.

Remember, your baby dragon is a special companion who will always be by your side, ready for adventure! Take good care of it, and you’ll have a lifelong friend in the world of imagination.
